At WE UP, we offer comprehensive services designed to identify, develop, and connect talented African footballers with international opportunities. Our three core services work together to provide a complete pathway from grassroots discovery to professional placement.

Player Scouting & Evaluation

Our experienced scouts identify promising talent across Ghana and West Africa. We conduct thorough evaluations of technical skills, physical attributes, tactical awareness, and mental strength. Using professional assessment criteria, we provide detailed player profiles and recommendations. Our scouting network covers local leagues, academies, and grassroots programs to ensure no talent goes unnoticed.

Training & Development

We provide specialized training programs designed to prepare players for international standards. Our programs focus on technical refinement, tactical understanding, physical conditioning and mental preparation. We work with certified coaches who bring international experience and proven methodologies. Our goal is to bridge the gap between local talent and global expectations, ensuring players are ready for the next level.

International Placements

We connect talented players with clubs, academies, and trials across Europe, Asia, and the Americas. Our extensive network includes professional clubs, agents, and football federations worldwide. We handle all aspects of the placement process, including negotiations, documentation, and travel arrangements. Our track record demonstrates our commitment to finding the right opportunities for each player's career.
